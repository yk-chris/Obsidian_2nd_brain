# 00388.HK — Spot Trading（60.63%）

## 成本結構分析

Spot trading represents the largest revenue contributor at HKD 14.4 billion in FY2025[1]. As the core cash equities trading business, this segment benefits from the exchange's role as a marketplace operator. Operating margins remain exceptionally strong with the company-wide operating margin at 60.7%[3], supported by high EBITDA margins of 81% in Q1 2026[2]. The primary cost drivers include technology infrastructure for matching engines, clearing systems, and regulatory compliance personnel dedicated to surveillance and market integrity.

## 投資回報分析（ROI / ROIC）

Spot trading demonstrates exceptional returns with gross margin at 78.74%[3] and net margin at 74.53%[3]. The business model exhibits high scalability—Q1 2026 saw core business income rise 22% compared to Q1 2025, driven by higher trading volumes in the spot market and increased trading and clearing fees[2]. With minimal incremental infrastructure costs per additional trade, ROI remains substantial. The segment's profitability is largely variable-cost dependent, allowing for significant leverage during high-volume trading periods.

## 成長驅動因素

Spot trading growth is driven by:
- **Trading volume expansion**: Q1 2026 demonstrated 22% core business income growth reflecting elevated market activity[2]
- **Market capitalization growth**: Hong Kong equity market development and increased participation
- **Geopolitical factors**: Regional capital flows to Hong Kong as a gateway between mainland China and international markets
- **Future outlook**: Estimated 15.88% total revenue growth rate over 3-5 years[3]

## 競爭地位

As Hong Kong's sole equities exchange operator, 00388.HK maintains monopolistic competitive positioning. Revenue concentration in spot trading (60.63%) reflects the exchange's dominant market position with no direct competitors in Hong Kong equities trading. However, competitive pressures exist from:
- Regional exchanges (Shanghai, Shenzhen, Singapore)
- International trading venues offering Hong Kong-listed securities
- Over-the-counter trading platforms

The segment's sustained revenue growth and margin expansion indicate continued market dominance and pricing power.
