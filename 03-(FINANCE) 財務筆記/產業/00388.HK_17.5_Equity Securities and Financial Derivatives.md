# 00388.HK — Equity Securities and Financial Derivatives（17.52%）

## 成本結構分析

This segment generated HKD 4.16 billion in FY2025[1], representing the second-largest revenue stream. Derivative trading requires sophisticated risk management, clearing, and settlement infrastructure. Cost structure includes dedicated personnel for derivatives clearing, margin management systems, and regulatory compliance specific to derivatives markets. The segment benefits from the same high-margin operational leverage as spot trading, with company-wide gross margins at 78.74%[3], as derivative trades utilize shared technology infrastructure with minimal variable-cost increments.

## 投資回報分析（ROI / ROIC）

Derivatives trading exhibits strong profitability characteristics with operating margins of 60.7%[3] and net margins of 74.53%[3]. The business model capitalizes on clearing fees, trading fees, and market data subscriptions from derivatives participants. Q1 2026 results indicate robust performance with 22% core business income growth[2], suggesting derivatives trading benefited significantly from elevated market volatility and hedging demand. Fee-based revenue generation creates predictable, scalable returns with high capital efficiency.

## 成長驅動因素

Growth drivers for derivatives include:
- **Volatility expansion**: Market turbulence increases hedging demand and derivatives usage
- **Index futures and options**: Expansion of derivative products on various underlying indices
- **Institutional participation**: Growing use of derivatives by fund managers and hedging entities
- **Product innovation**: New derivative contracts targeting specific market segments
- **Regional integration**: Cross-border derivatives trading with mainland China and international participants

## 競爭地位

The derivatives segment faces moderate competition from regional exchanges offering competing contracts and international venues. However, 00388.HK maintains significant advantages through:
- Deep liquidity in Hong Kong equity and index futures
- Regulatory recognition and market infrastructure leadership
- Integration with cash equities markets for arbitrage and hedging
- Dominance in Hang Seng Index derivatives contracts

The segment represents a natural extension of core equities trading with complementary market participants.
