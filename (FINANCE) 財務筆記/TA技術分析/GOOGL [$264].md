- 後五年：降速至10%；終值永續增長率3%
    
- 淨現金：+$95B（加回股權價值）[](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/alphabets-strong-free-cash-flow-140002947.html)

- META：PE ~23x、EV/EBITDA ~16x[](https://multiples.vc/public-comps/amazon-valuation-multiples)

4. 資本開支持續攀升（AI基建）對FCF margin的壓縮程度
